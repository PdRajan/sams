About the Achievement Management System

The Achievement Management System (AMS) is a web portal for the recording and management of achievements of students and faculty of Maharaja Surajmal Institute at a single centralized location.

Students and faculty alike submit their achievements and other feats through HTML forms on this portal, and these achievements are then put on display once they have been verified by the faculty and admin of the institute.

Why use an online digital achievement management system?

+

This Achievement Management System web portal was created as a way to alleviate the burden of manually recording achievements on the faculty and administration of Maharja Surajmal Institute, and instead automate the process. This is a sorely needed facility, as the students and faculty at MSI get a plethora of prestigious achievements in a single semester.

What are the main features of the AMS?

+

Through the AMS, students and faculties can submit their achievements. Faculties can verify student's achievements in order to ensure that the correct details are recorded on the web page. Once the achievement and its details have been verified, they will be visible to everyone through the student or faculty's profile page.

What are the advantages of the AMS?

+

The main advantages of the AMS are:

Centralized storage and access of user records

Ease of use due to a user interface designed with ease-of-use in mind

Built in verification checks for data to ensure that only authentic data is recorded.

The web page is built using Django and lightweight CSS, making it fast on any device, from smartphones to powerful computers.
